Hotel Levitan 

   Located in Vladimir, 200 meters from Assumption Cathedral,
 Hotel Levitan provides accommodation with a restaurant, free private parking,
a bar and a shared lounge. Among the facilities at this property are a 24-hour
 front desk and room service, along with free WiFi throughout the property.
 The property features evening entertainment and a tour desk.
 At the hotel all rooms are fitted with a wardrobe, a flat-screen TV,
 a private bathroom, bed linen and towels.The units feature a safety deposit box.
  Guests at Levitan Hotel can enjoy a continental or a buffet breakfast.
  Popular points of interest near the accommodation include Palaty Museum,
    Nativity Monastery and Gingerbread . Museum
Hotel Ekaterina Kostroma 

  This property is a 6-minute walk from the beach. Located in Kostroma,
 1.7 km from Epiphany Monastery, Hotel Ekaterina Kostroma provides accommodation
 with a restaurant,free private parking, a bar and a shared lounge.
 This 4-star hotel offers room service and a business centre.
 The property provides evening entertainment and a 24-hour front desk.
 At the hotel all rooms come with a wardrobe,aflat-screen TV and a private bathroom.
 The units will provide guests with a refrigerator.
 Guests at Ekaterina Kostroma Hotel can enjoy a buffet breakfast.
   The property offers a terrace. The area is popular for hiking and skiing,
    and bike rental is available at Hotel Ekaterina Kostroma.
Radisson Collection Paradise 

 Located in Adler and on the Black Sea coast, Radisson Collection Paradise Resort
    and Spa Sochi provides a number of facilities to ensure a comfortable stay.
    These facilities include a 2,500 square meter spa and wellness centre,
    indoor and outdoor pools,children's playground and multiple restaurants on site.
    The Olympicand motor racing circuit are within a 10-minute walk of this hotel.
    The spacious at this property feature individually controlled air conditioning,
    Wi-Fi, electronic safety deposit box, minibar and tea-making facilities.
    Bathrooms come with a shower or bathtub.
    Guests can enjoy grilled Asian food at Umami Restaurant.
      There are also a number of pool bars,
      Lounge Bar, Voices Karaoke Club and Oak Tree Bar.
