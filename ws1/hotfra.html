Hotel Bradford Elysees - Astotel 

 Located in an exceptional area between the Champs-Elysees and Faubourg St-Honore,
    discover the charm of a authentic Haussman-style hotel.
    Surrounded by the portraits of the Bradford family,
    presented in a universe of soft and warm silks,
    let yourself be seduced by the refined,
    bright and delicate interior of Bradford Elysees.
    All rooms are serviced by a magnificient historical lift.
    All rooms are air conditioned and decorated in classic, elegant colors.
    Some of our 50 rooms feature house chimneys

Hotel Royal-Riviera 

Perched in the peninsula of Cap-Ferrat,
    with plunging views of the Provencal gardens and the Mediterranean,
    the 94-room Hotel Royal-Riviera provides the intimacy
    of a private villa with the luxury and glamour of a palace.
    L'Orangerie villa's 16 rooms and suites each offer
    a large terrace overlooking the garden or the pool, or a balcony.
    Guests may dine in the restaurant "La Table du Royal"
    or on its outdoor terrace, overlooking the Mediterranean Sea,
    or during the season at the poolside restaurant “Jasmin Grill & Lounge”
